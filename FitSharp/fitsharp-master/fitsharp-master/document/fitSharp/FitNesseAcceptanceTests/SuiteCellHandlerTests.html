NOTE: These will only work for ColumnFixture, though they may be supported in RowFixture in a future release.

CellHandlers allow FitNesse to figure out how to handle a cell based on certain characters or keywords in that cell. In Column Fixtures, the text in the column header will determine whether to input new data or examine existing data (Test Query Symbols).

The individual cells in a column will be handled in different ways depending on the patterns that exist in the cells. There are default handlers (Suite Default Cell Handler Tests), optional handlers that are available in FitNesse (Suite Optional And Custom Handler Tests) and a mechanism for adding your own handlers (Suite Optional And Custom Handler Tests).
